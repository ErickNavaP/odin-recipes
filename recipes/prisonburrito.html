Prison Burrito

Description: Full of flavor, light but filling

Burritos! When we think of burritos, we think of a filling flavorful Tex-Mex dish which makes our mouths water just thinking about it. In this recipe you will learn to make a burrito but using unconventional ingredients which will delightfully surprise you. The dish you make will be fluffy and fresh but with enough flavor to make ask for more.Aside from being surprisingly delicious, it is remarkably economic.

Ingredients

  • flour tortillas
  • cheese (whichever your prefer but for better experience, of the squeesable kind works best)
  • 3 chili flavored ramen noodles (square ones in plastic packaging)
  • summer sausage
  • 1 cup of instant white rice
  • refried beans (can or instant)
  • jalapeno slices
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • bag of chips (which ever you prefer but recommended ones are: Doritos, or The Whole Shabang)

Steps

  1. In three seperate containers, place your noodles, rice and beans
  2. Boil water and add to each
  3. In frying pan saute your summer sausage
  4. While summer sausage is sauteing, drain excess water from noodle, rice and beans
  5. Add chili packs to your noodles
  6. Remove summer sausage from pan to no burn it
  7. Mix noodles, rice, summer sausage, diced onion and bell pepper in a bowl
  8. Place a tortilla on frying pan for 30 seconds
  9. Remove and proceed to add a spoonfull of beans to tortilla
  10. Next add a cup of your noodle mix
  11. Squirt some squeeze cheese on to of it all
  12. Add jalapeno slices
  13. As a side dish, add some of your choosen ships or crunch up the chips and add to the inside of burrito
  14. BON APPETIT!
