Gringas

Description: Crisp, Flavorful, and Filling

Gringas. It is okay if you don't know what a gringa is. You will after today. This recipe is simple. The ingredients you will use are ones that every Mexican household has but are common enough that you will have them too! This recipe can be executed in a short amount of time, giving you an option for a quick lunch or dinner opiton. It can even be used as a compliment to other meals, like soups.

Ingredients

  • flower tortillas
  • chihuahua cheese (or motzarella
  • ground beef
  • onions
  • avacado
  • lettuce
  • jalapenos
  • lime
  • cooking oil
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. On a fying pan, add a teaspoon of oil
  2. Add the ground beef and cover
  3. After a few minutes, stir and add salt and pepper to taste
  4. Cook until meat is crispy brown
  5. Remove meat from pan and place back on flame
  6. Place one tortilla on pan and add a five finger pinch of cheese
  7. Once cheese starts to melt, add 1/4 cup of cooked ground beef
  8. Add sliced unions, avacado, and jalapeno slices
  9. Cover with another tortilla and flip accordingly
  10. Once tortilla is golden brown, serve on plate
  11. Open gringa and add diced lettuce and lime
