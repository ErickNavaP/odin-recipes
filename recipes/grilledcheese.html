Grilled Cheese

Description: Crispy, Cheesy, Perfect

Grilled cheese. Who doesn't like grilled cheese, right? This recipe is simple. It requires minimal inredients, three to be exact! This recipe can be executed in a matter of minutes, giving you an enjoyable mid-day snack. It can even be used as a compliment to other meals, like soups.

Ingredients

  • sliced bred
  • cheddar cheese
  • butter

Steps

  1. Add butter to one side of each slice of bread
  2. Put the slices of bread, buttered side down, on the hot skillet
  3. After one minute add a slice of cheese on each slice of bread
  4. Wait 30 seconds then put one slice of bread over another, cheese touching
  5. Flip acoordingly or until toasty!
